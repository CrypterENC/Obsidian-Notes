### The 6 Core Functions

The framework is organized into six main functions. **~={pink}Govern is the new central function=~** added in version 2.0 that **~={cyan}informs all the others.=~**

---

#### Identify (ID)

- **Role:** i.e. **~={cyan}they understand=~** the **~={pink}current cybersecurity risks=~** to the organization.
    
	- They perform **~={red}Asset Management ID.AM=~** i.e. **~={cyan}they identify and manage=~** **~={pink}hardware, software, data, and services=~** based on **~={cyan}their importance.=~**
	    
	- They conduct ~={red}**Risk Assessment (ID.RA)=~:** i.e. **~={cyan}they understand risks=~** to **~={pink}assets, individuals, and the organization.=~**

---

#### Protect (PR)

- **Role:** i.e. **~={cyan}they use ~={orange}safeguards=~**=~ to **~={pink}manage the organization's cybersecurity risks.=~**
    
	- They manage **~={red}Access Control (PR.AA)=~:** i.e. **~={cyan}they implement protections=~** for **~={pink}identity management and access control.=~**
	    
	- They conduct **~={red}Awareness & Training (PR.AT)=~** i.e. **~={cyan}they empower staff=~** through **~={pink}security awareness and role-based training.=~**
	    
	- They ensure ~={red}**Data Security (PR.DS)=~:** i.e. **~={cyan}they establish safeguards=~** to protect **~={pink}confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.=~**

---
#### Detect (DE)

- **Role:** i.e. **~={cyan}they find and analyze=~** **~={pink}possible cybersecurity attacks and compromises.=~**
    
	- They perform~={red} **Continuous Monitoring=~ ([DE.CM](https://de.cm/)):** i.e. **~={cyan}they monitor systems and networks=~** to **~={pink}detect cybersecurity events and verify protective measures.=~**
	    
	- They conduct ~={red}**Adverse Event Analysis=~ ([DE.AE](https://de.ae/)):** i.e. **~={cyan}they ensure anomalies are detected=~** and **~={pink}their potential impact is understood.=~**

---

#### Respond (RS)

- **Role:** i.e. **~={cyan}they take action=~** regarding a **~={pink}detected cybersecurity incident.=~**
    
	- They manage ~={red}**Incident Management=~ ([RS.MA](https://rs.ma/)):** i.e. **~={cyan}they execute response plans=~** **~={pink}during and after an incident.=~**
	    
	- They perform ~={red}**Incident Analysis=~ ([RS.AN](https://rs.an/)):** i.e. **~={cyan}they analyze incidents=~** to **~={pink}ensure effective response and support recovery.=~**
	    
	- They conduct ~={red}**Incident Mitigation=~ (RS.MI):** i.e. **~={cyan}they perform activities=~** to **~={pink}prevent expansion of an event and resolve the incident.=~**

---

#### Recover (RC)

- **Role:** i.e. **~={cyan}they restore=~** **~={pink}assets and operations=~** affected by a cybersecurity incident.
    
	- They execute ~={red}**Recovery Plan=~ (RC.RP):** i.e. **~={cyan}they implement procedures=~** to **~={pink}restore systems and assets affected by incidents.=~**
	    
	- They coordinate ~={red}**Recovery Communication =~([RC.CO](https://rc.co/)):** i.e. **~={cyan}they coordinate internal and external communications=~** **~={pink}during and after recovery.=~**
