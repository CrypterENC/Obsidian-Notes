- ~={yellow}**Linux=~** is a **~={green}part of several platforms=~** and can be found on devices anywhere from wristwatches to supercomputers.

- Linux is often the OS of choice in the SOC. These are some of the reasons to choose Linux:

		- Anyone can acquire Linux at no charge and modify it to fit there specific needs
	2. ~={yellow}**Linux's CLI is very powerful.**=~
		- Then Linux CLI is extremely powerful and enables analyst to preform task not only directly on a terminal, but also remotely
	3. ~={yellow}**The user has more control over the OS.**=~
